## What each market tracks

Each market tracks the average **price per square foot** of residential real estate in that geography, updated continuously by the Shareland oracle. The oracle aggregates recent home sales, active listings, and historical trends to produce a live $/sqft price.

## How markets are added

New markets go through a validation process before launch:

1. **Data review** - sufficient transaction volume must exist for reliable oracle pricing
2. **Oracle validation** - prices are backtested and validated by Shareland's data team (led by Andrey Pavlov, PhD)
3. **Launch** - market goes live with initial liquidity
