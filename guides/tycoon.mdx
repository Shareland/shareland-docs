# Tycoon AI Agent

Tycoon is Shareland's built-in AI agent - your personal real estate investment analyst, available directly on the exchange.

## What Tycoon does

Tycoon gives you real-time intelligence to make smarter trading decisions:

- **Market insights** - analysis of neighborhood trends, price movements, and oracle data
- **Portfolio analysis** - understand your current positions and exposure
- **Data synthesis** - combines oracle data, transaction history, and demographic information
- **Trade assistance** - helps you understand the implications of a trade before you make it

## How to access Tycoon

Tycoon is available directly within the Shareland exchange. Look for the Tycoon AI interface in the exchange navigation.

  Start trading with Tycoon AI on the Shareland exchange
</Card>

## The TYCOON token

`$TYCOON` is the utility token associated with the Tycoon AI agent within the [Virtuals Protocol](https://virtuals.io) ecosystem. Transaction fees on the Shareland exchange flow into a buyback and burn program for `$TYCOON` holders, aligning incentives between the platform and token holders.
