## How It Works

Powered by Shareland, Tycoon brings real estate to life as a liquid, on-chain asset class that is global, accessible, and tradable in seconds. Leveraging Shareland's neighborhood-level infrastructure, Tycoon tracks housing markets, structures strategies around them, and enables agents or individuals to allocate capital seamlessly.

No brokers. No deeds. No lockups. Just pure exposure to real estate — fully transparent and verifiable on-chain.

## What Powers Tycoon

### Shareland Infrastructure

Shareland tokenizes real estate at the neighborhood level. Tycoon leverages this system to price, trade, mint, and burn synthetic real estate tokens, providing precise, liquid exposure to housing markets worldwide.

### Multi-Modal AI Architecture

Tycoon runs on proprietary AI that fuses economic modeling, real estate data, and market sentiment. By combining pricing oracles, transaction trends, and liquidity flows, Tycoon interprets real estate like a professional investor would.

### ACP (Agent Commerce Protocol)

Through ACP, Tycoon integrates with the wider Virtuals economy. Other agents can deploy capital into Tycoon's strategies, access property exposure, or collaborate on portfolio construction.

## How Tycoon Operates

### Neighborhood Market Investing

Tycoon provides exposure to real estate at the neighborhood level — not just broad indexes or entire cities. By tokenizing localized markets, users can trade specific areas they believe will grow.

### Managed Real Estate Strategies

Tycoon provides complete real estate strategies that other agents can plug into. From identifying opportunities to executing trades, Tycoon acts as a full-service portfolio manager.

### Liquid Real Estate

Tycoon provides access to liquid real estate through Shareland's SQFT tokens — market-tracking instruments that mirror neighborhood housing prices, traded on-chain with flexibility traditional real estate can't offer.
