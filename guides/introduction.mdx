# Welcome to Shareland

Shareland is the first platform that lets anyone invest in real estate by the square foot — no minimum investment, no lockups, and fully liquid.

## What makes Shareland different

Traditional real estate investing requires hundreds of thousands of dollars, years of commitment, and trusted brokers. Shareland changes that.

| Traditional RE | Shareland |
|---|---|
| $50,000+ minimum | No minimum |
| Months to buy/sell | Instant trades |
| High fees (6%) | 1.5% fee |
| One property | Any neighborhood |

## The Square Foot Token (SQFT)

The SQFT token is a synthetic asset that tracks the price per square foot of residential real estate in any geography. When a neighborhood's home prices rise, your SQFT tokens go up in value.
