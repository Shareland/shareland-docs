# Community Tab

Each market on Shareland has a community tab where traders can post messages about that neighborhood or market.

## How to access it

1. From the exchange, click any market card to open the detail panel
2. In the panel, select the **Community** tab

You'll see messages posted by other traders in that market.

## Posting

Type your message and post directly from the community tab. Keep posts relevant to the market.

<Note>
  Be respectful and stay on topic. Shareland reserves the right to remove posts that violate community standards.
</Note>
