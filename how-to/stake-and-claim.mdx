# Stake and Claim Rewards

Shareland offers rewards for participating in the ecosystem.

## Staking

Staking allows you to earn rewards by locking your SQFT tokens or providing liquidity.

1.  **Navigate to Stake:** Find the "Stake" section in your portfolio or market page.
2.  **Select Asset:** Choose the SQFT token or liquidity pair you want to stake.
3.  **Confirm:** Approve the transaction to deposit your tokens into the staking contract.

## Unstaking

To retrieve your tokens:

1.  **Select Unstake:** Choose the staked position you want to withdraw.
2.  **Wait Period:** Some staking pools may have a lock-up period. Check the pool details.
3.  **Confirm:** Approve the transaction to withdraw your tokens back to your wallet.

## Claiming Rewards

Rewards accumulate over time based on your staked amount and the pool's APR.

1.  **Check Rewards:** View your accrued rewards in the staking dashboard.
2.  **Claim:** Click the "Claim" button to transfer rewards to your wallet.
3.  **Gas Fee:** You will need to pay a small gas fee to execute the claim transaction.

<Note>
  **Rewards Distribution:** Rewards are typically distributed in USDC or platform tokens, depending on the specific program.
</Note>
