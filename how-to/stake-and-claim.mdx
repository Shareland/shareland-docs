---
title: Stake, Unstake, and Claim
description: How to stake tokens and claim rewards on Shareland.
---

Staking deposits your SQFT tokens into a liquidity pool, where they help facilitate trades on the exchange. In return, you receive LP (liquidity provider) tokens and earn USDC rewards from trading fees. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed at any time.

## How to Stake

### Step 1: Open a market

From the exchange, click any market card to open the detail panel.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Markets">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/markets.png" alt="Shareland markets view" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Open the Stake tab

In the market detail panel, click **Trade** then select the **Stake** tab.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Stake panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/stake.png" alt="Stake panel" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Enter your amount

Enter the number of SQFT tokens you want to stake. The panel shows your current staking position and estimated rewards.

### Step 4: Confirm

Review and confirm the transaction. You'll receive LP tokens representing your share of the liquidity pool. Your position is visible in your Portfolio.

## How to Unstake

### Step 1: Open the Unstake tab

Navigate to the market where you have staked tokens and select the **Unstake** tab.

### Step 2: Enter the amount

Enter the number of SQFT tokens you want to withdraw from staking.

### Step 3: Confirm

Approve the transaction to withdraw your tokens back to your wallet.

## How to Claim Rewards

Rewards accumulate over time based on your staked amount.

1.  **Check Rewards:** View your accrued rewards in the **Portfolio** tab under **Staking Rewards**.
2.  **Claim:** Click the **Claim** button to transfer USDC rewards to your wallet.

<Note>
  Staking rewards are distributed in USDC and can be claimed at any time from your Portfolio.
</Note>
