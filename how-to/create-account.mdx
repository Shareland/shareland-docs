# Create an Account

<Frame caption="Shareland exchange — Sign Up flow">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/create-account.png" alt="Shareland sign up" />
</Frame>

## Step 1: Go to share.land

Visit [share.land](https://share.land) and click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.

## Step 2: Connect your wallet

Shareland uses [Privy](https://privy.io) for secure wallet management. You can sign up with:
- **Email** - Privy creates an embedded wallet for you automatically
- **External wallet** - connect MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

<Note>
  No prior crypto experience needed. Email sign-up creates a wallet automatically — you don't need to manage keys.
</Note>

## Step 3: Verify your email

Enter the 6-digit code sent to your email to verify your account.

## Step 4: Complete your profile

Before trading, complete your profile:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Accept the Terms of Service and risk disclosures

<Warning>
  Profile completion is required before you can buy or sell SQFT tokens. This is a compliance requirement.
</Warning>

## Step 5: Start exploring

Once your profile is complete, you're ready to browse markets and make your first trade.
