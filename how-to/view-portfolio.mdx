# View Your Portfolio

Navigate to **Portfolio** in the top nav to see your full investment dashboard.

## Portfolio tabs

| Tab | What you'll find |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Assets** | All your open SQFT positions and their current value |
| **Watchlist** | Markets you're tracking but haven't invested in yet |
| **Activity** | Full history of your buy, sell, and reward transactions |

## What's on the dashboard

**User Profile** — your wallet address and display name. Click **Edit** to update.

**Portfolio Value** — total current value of all your open positions in USDC, at live oracle prices. Updates in real time.

**Renting Rewards** — protocol revenue distributed to your wallet. Click **Claim** to withdraw at any time.

**Buying Power** — USDC available in your wallet to invest.

## Reading your positions

Each position in the **Assets** tab shows:
- **Market** — which SQFT token you hold (e.g. SQFT\_NYC)
- **Current value** — position value in USDC at the live oracle price
- **Gain/loss** — unrealized P&L since your entry

## Transaction history

The **Activity** tab shows a full log of every buy, sell, mint, burn, and reward claim — with timestamps, amounts, and fees.
