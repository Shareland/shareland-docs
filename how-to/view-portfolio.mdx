# View Your Portfolio

Navigate to **Portfolio** in the top nav to see your full trading dashboard.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Portfolio dashboard">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/portfolio.png" alt="Portfolio dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Portfolio tabs

| Tab | What you'll find |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Assets** | All your open SQFT positions and their current value |
| **Watchlist** | Markets you're tracking but haven't traded yet |
| **Activity** | Full history of every buy, sell, and reward transaction |

## What's on the dashboard

**Portfolio Value** - total current value of all open positions in USDC, at live oracle prices. Updates in real time.

**Renting Rewards** - protocol revenue distributed to your wallet. Click **Claim** to withdraw at any time.

**Buying Power** - USDC available in your wallet to trade.

## Reading your positions

Each position in the **Assets** tab shows:
- **Market** - which SQFT token you hold (e.g. SQFT\_NYC)
- **Current value** - position value in USDC at the live oracle price
- **Gain/loss** - unrealized P&L since your entry

## Transaction history

The **Activity** tab shows a full log of every trade, mint, burn, and reward claim — with timestamps, amounts, and fees paid.
