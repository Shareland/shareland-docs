# Borrow Against Your Position

Shareland lets you borrow USDC against your SQFT token holdings — unlocking liquidity without closing your position.

## Step 1: Open a market

From the exchange, click any market card to open the action panel. You can also access this from your Portfolio by clicking on an existing position.

## Step 2: Open the Borrow tab

In the action panel, select the **Borrow** tab.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Borrow panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/borrow.png" alt="Borrow panel" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Enter your amount

Enter the USDC amount you want to borrow. The panel shows your available collateral value and how much you can safely borrow.

## Collateralization

Shareland uses a **Minimum Collateralization Ratio (MCR) of 110%**. This means for every $110 of collateral value, you can borrow up to $100 USDC.

We recommend maintaining at least **120% collateralization** to give yourself a buffer against price movements.

<Warning>
  If your collateralization ratio drops to 110%, your position will be automatically liquidated to repay the loan. Monitor your position regularly, especially during volatile markets.
</Warning>

## Step 4: Confirm

Review the terms and confirm. Borrowed USDC is sent to your connected wallet immediately.

## Repaying

To repay a borrow position and reclaim your full collateral, return to the Borrow tab and repay the outstanding USDC balance.
