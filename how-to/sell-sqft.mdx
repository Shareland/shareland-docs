# Sell SQFT Tokens

## Step 1: Open your portfolio

Navigate to **Portfolio** in the top nav. Your open positions are listed in the **Assets** tab.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Portfolio Assets tab">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/portfolio.png" alt="Portfolio assets tab" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Select a position

Click on the position you want to exit. You'll see your current value, entry price, and unrealized gain/loss.

## Step 3: Enter your sell order

Click **Trade** then select the **Sell** tab. Enter the amount to close — either a specific number of tokens or a USDC value. Partial exits are fully supported.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Sell trade panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/trade-sell.png" alt="Trade panel — sell" />
</Frame>

## Step 4: Confirm

Review the transaction details — including the 1.5% fee — and confirm. Funds return to your connected wallet immediately after the transaction confirms on-chain.

<Note>
  You can sell any portion of a position. You don't have to exit all at once.
</Note>
