# Points and Rewards

Shareland runs a loyalty program at [points.share.land](https://points.share.land) where you earn points for contributing to the community. Points accumulate across multiple activity types and update daily.

## Season 1

Season 1 is live now. Earn points by completing quests, engaging on social media, and trading on the exchange. Top performers on the leaderboard compete for prizes.

**Leaderboard:** [testnet.share.land/leaderboard](https://testnet.share.land/leaderboard)

## How to Earn Points

### `$TYCOON` Holders

Hold `$TYCOON` tokens to earn **10+ points per day**. Rewards scale by tier based on how much `$TYCOON` you hold.

Get `$TYCOON` at [app.virtuals.io/geneses/8699](https://app.virtuals.io/geneses/8699).

### Community and Growth

| Quest | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Register on the Shareland Exchange | 25 (unlocks quests) |
| Follow Tycoon on X | 5 |
| Follow Shareland on X | 5 |
| Join Shareland's Discord | 5 |
| Refer a friend | Bonus |

### Social Engagement

| Activity | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Post about Shareland on X | 5 / day |
| Post about Tycoon on X | 5 / day |
| Comment on X with `#shareland` | 2 / day |
| Like the pinned X post | 1 |
| Repost the pinned X post | 1 |

### On-Chain Quests and Weekly Contests

Complete on-chain quests and participate in weekly contests to earn additional points. Awards go to the best performers and top liquidity providers each week.

## Staking Rewards

In addition to the loyalty program, you earn **Staking Rewards** (in USDC) when you stake SQFT tokens on the exchange. These are separate from loyalty points and are visible in your Portfolio tab.
