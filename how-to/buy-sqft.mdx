# Buy SQFT Tokens

## Step 1: Browse markets

From the exchange, browse the available markets. Each card shows the current oracle price ($/sqft), 24h volume, and recent price movement.

<Frame caption="Shareland exchange — Explore Markets">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/markets.png" alt="Shareland markets view" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Select a market

Click any market card to open the detail panel on the right. You'll see:
- Live price chart and oracle price
- Neighborhood breakdown with individual area prices
- Community posts from other traders
- Your current position in this market (if any)

<Frame caption="Shareland — Market detail panel (New York)">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/market-detail.png" alt="Market detail panel" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Open the trade panel

In the market detail panel, click **Trade** to open the buy/sell interface.

## Step 4: Enter your amount

Select the **Buy** tab. Enter the USDC amount you want to trade. The interface shows you:
- Number of SQFT tokens you'll receive
- 1.5% transaction fee
- Total cost

**Minimum:** $0.10 per trade. No maximum.

<Frame caption="Shareland — Buy trade panel">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/trade.png" alt="Trade panel — buy" />
</Frame>

## Step 5: Confirm

Review the details and confirm. The transaction settles on-chain in seconds — your position appears in your portfolio immediately.

<Check>
  Your new position is visible instantly in the Portfolio tab.
</Check>
