# Buy SQFT Tokens

## Step 1 — Browse markets

From the exchange, browse available markets. Each market card shows the current price ($/sqft), 24h volume, and 1-month price change.

<img src="/images/screenshots/markets.png" alt="Shareland exchange — market list" />

## Step 2 — Select a market

Click on any market card to open the detail view. You'll see:
- Live price and price chart
- Historical oracle data
- Neighborhood breakdown and demographics
- Community posts from other investors

## Step 3 — Open the trade panel

In the market detail panel, click **Trade** to open the buy/sell interface.

## Step 4 — Enter your amount

Select the **Buy** tab and enter the amount you'd like to invest in USDC. The interface shows:
- Number of SQFT tokens you'll receive
- 1.5% transaction fee
- Total cost

**Minimum:** $0.10 per transaction. No maximum.

## Step 5 — Confirm

Review the details and confirm your trade. The transaction processes on-chain in a few seconds.
