# Regulatory & Legal

## Risk Disclosure

Trading SQFT tokens involves significant risk. Real estate prices can go down as well as up. You may lose some or all of your funds. Do not trade more than you can afford to lose.

## Nature of SQFT Tokens

SQFT tokens are synthetic derivative instruments designed to track the price per square foot of residential real estate in specific neighborhoods. They do not represent ownership of real property, shares in a property, or any security. There is no right of redemption against Shareland or any property owner.
